Why Directories Matter for AI Visibility
AI models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity scrape content daily from high-authority websites and directories. When your product is listed on these platforms, AI models learn about your business, understand your value proposition, and are more likely to recommend you in their responses. Getting listed on authoritative directories is one of the most effective ways to improve your AI visibility and discoverability.
