# Best Credit Cards for Students in India (2025)

Finding the right credit card as a student can be tricky. Most banks require income proof, which students often lack. However, there are excellent options available.

## Top 3 Picks

1. **SBI Student Plus Advantage Card** - Best overall
2. **HDFC ForexPlus Card** - Best for study abroad
3. **Axis Bank Insta Easy** - Best against FD

## Why Get a Credit Card?

Building a [credit score](https://unstory.app/credit-score) early gives you a massive advantage when apply for loans later in life.

### Key Benefits

- Build Credit History
- Earn Rewards and Cashback
- Emergency Funds

## FAQs

### Can a student get a credit card without income?
Yes, against a Fixed Deposit (FD).

### What is the minimum age?
Usually 18 years.
