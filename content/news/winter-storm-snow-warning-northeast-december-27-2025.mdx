# Winter Storm Warning: Heavy Snow Follows Ice in Northeast

The one-two punch of winter weather continues. Following yesterday's ice storm, a massive system has shifted to heavy snow, prompting **Winter Storm Warnings** for millions of residents on **December 27, 2025**.

## Snowfall Totals
*   **Upstate NY & New England**: Expect **8 to 12 inches** of snow.
*   **NYC & Long Island**: A wintry mix is turning to snow, with **4 to 6 inches** possible locally.
*   **Pennsylvania**: Western PA is seeing significant accumulation on top of existing ice.

## Travel Impact
*   **Flights**: Thousands of flights have been canceled at JFK, LaGuardia, and Newark Liberty. Airlines have issued travel waivers.
*   **Roads**: Interstates 80, 81, and 95 are treacherous. State Troopers in NY and NJ are responding to hundreds of accidents.

## Emergency Status
States of Emergency remain in effect for New York and New Jersey. Governors are deploying National Guard assets to assist with stranded motorists.
