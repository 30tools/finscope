# Remembering Imani Dia Smith: "Lion King" Star Dies at 25

The Broadway community is mourning the tragic loss of **Imani Dia Smith**, best known for her role as "Young Nala" in *The Lion King*. She passed away in December 2025 at the young age of 25.

## Incident Details
Smith was found with fatal stab wounds at a residence in Edison, New Jersey, on December 21. She was transported to Robert Wood Johnson University Hospital where she succumbed to her injuries.

## Investigation & Arrest
Police have arrested her former partner, **Jordan D. Jackson-Small** (35). He has been charged with:
*   First-degree murder
*   Child endangerment
*   Weapons offenses

## Legacy
Imani Dia Smith performed on Broadway from 2011 to 2012, touching the hearts of thousands. She leaves behind a 3-year-old son, her parents, and two siblings. A GoFundMe has been set up to support her son's future.
