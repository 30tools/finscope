# Laredo News Recap: December 2025

Several significant incidents marked the holiday season in Laredo, Texas, alongside updates on major infrastructure projects.

## Crime & Safety
*   **Christmas Morning Shooting**: Police responded to a shooting in the **4200 block of Exodus Drive**. An argument at a holiday gathering escalated, leading to an arrest.
*   **Human Smuggling Pursuit**: On Christmas Eve, a high-speed chase involving suspected human smugglers ended in a crash and arrest.
*   **8th Murder of 2025**: Earlier in the month (Dec 7), Laredo recorded its 8th homicide of the year.

## Fire Incident
*   **Market Street Fire**: Laredo Fire Department quickly contained a structure fire on Market Street. Fortunately, no injuries were reported.

## Infrastructure Progress
*   **Loop 20 Expansion**: Amidst the news, city officials confirmed that the **Loop 20 highway expansion project** is now nearly **40% complete**, promising better traffic flow for the future.
