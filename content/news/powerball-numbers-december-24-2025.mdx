# Powerball Results: Christmas Eve Jackpot Hit for $1.8 Billion!

The holiday season brought a life-changing gift for one lucky lottery player. On the Christmas Eve drawing held on **December 24, 2025**, a single ticket sold in **Arkansas** matched all six numbers to win the staggering **$1.817 billion** jackpot.

## The Winning Numbers
Here are the winning numbers for the December 24, 2025 drawing:

*   **White Balls**: 4 - 25 - 31 - 52 - 59
*   **Red Powerball**: 19
*   **Power Play**: 2x

## Jackpot Details
This jackpot goes down in history as the **second-largest U.S. lottery jackpot ever**.
*   **Annuity Value**: $1.817 Billion
*   **Cash Option**: $834.9 Million (before taxes)

## Other Big Winners
Even if you didn't win the grand prize, check your tickets! There were millions of other winners across the country:
*   **Match 5 ($1 Million)**: 8 tickets matched all five white balls. These winners were located in:
    *   California
    *   Indiana
    *   Michigan
    *   New York (2 winners)
    *   Ohio
    *   Pennsylvania
    *   Virginia

## What to Do If You Won
If you are holding a winning ticket, sign the back immediately. Rules for claiming prizes vary by state, but jackpot winners typically have between 180 days and a year to come forward. Given the size of this prize, financial advisors recommend assembling a team of lawyers and accountants before heading to the lottery commission.
