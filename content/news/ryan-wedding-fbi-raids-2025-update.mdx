# From Olympian to Fugitive: The Hunt for Ryan Wedding Intensifies

The bizarre and dark saga of **Ryan Wedding** continued to unfold in late 2025. The former Canadian Olympic snowboarder, now an alleged drug kingpin, is the target of a massive international manhunt.

## The FBI Raids
In a coordinated strike, Mexican authorities and the FBI raided properties linked to Wedding's organization.
*   **The Haul**: Authorities seized 62 high-end motorcycles, vast quantities of methamphetamine, and even two Olympic medals (though confirmed not to be Wedding's own).
*   **The Charges**: Wedding is accused of running a billion-dollar drug trafficking empire that smuggles cocaine from Colombia through Mexico into the U.S. and Canada. He is also charged with ordering multiple murders.
