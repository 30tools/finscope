## What is Topical Authority?
It means covering a subject so thoroughly that search engines trust you as the definitive source.
*   *Weak:* A finance blog that writes one article about "Dog Training."
*   *Strong:* A Dog blog that has 500 articles covering every breed, food, and behavioral issue.

## The "Cluster" Strategy
To build this in 2026, you must think in **Clusters**, not Keywords.

### 1. The Pillar Page (The Hub)
A massive, 3,000-word guide covering the broad topic.
*   *Example:* "The Ultimate Guide to Keto Diet."

### 2. The Spoke Pages (The Detail)
20-30 articles linking back to the Pillar, answering specific questions.
*   *Examples:* "Is a Banana Keto?", "Keto Flu Symptoms," "Best Keto Starbucks Drinks."

### 3. Internal Linking (The Glue)
Every Spoke must link to the Pillar. The Pillar links to the Spokes. This tells Google: "We own this web of information."

## Why AI Can't Fake This
AI can write one article. But AI lacks the strategic foresight to plan a 50-article interconnected map that anticipates user intent.

## The Roadmap
1.  Pick **ONE** topic.
2.  List **50 questions** people ask about it.
3.  Answer them all.
4.  Link them together.
5.  **Do not deviate.**

This is how you beat the giants in 2026.
