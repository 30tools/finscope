# Boxing Day 2025: Retailers See Surge in Foot Traffic

**London, Dec 29, 2025:** Boxing Day (Dec 26) remains one of the pivotal shopping days for Commonwealth nations. Early reports from 2025 indicate a return to in-store shopping pre-pandemic levels.

## Regional Highlights
*   **United Kingdom:** High streets saw a **15% increase** in footfall compared to 2024. Next and Marks & Spencer reported long queues starting at 5 AM.
*   **Canada:** Best Buy Canada drove massive traffic with PS5 and OLED TV bundles.
*   **Australia:** Myer and David Jones held their traditional clearance events, with summer clothing being the top seller.

## Top Selling Categories
1.  **Consumer Electronics:** Consoles and Smart Home devices.
2.  **Winter Apparel:** (Northern Hemisphere) Coats and Boots.
3.  **Beauty:** Gift sets from Sephora and Boots were cleared out.
