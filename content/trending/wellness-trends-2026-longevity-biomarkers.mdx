At-home spit tests that tell you how fast you are aging are the new Fitbit.

## 2. Peptide Therapy Goes OTC
Peptides (chains of amino acids) like BPC-157 (for healing) and GHK-Cu (for skin) are moving from gray-market injections to mainstream creams and oral supplements.

*   **Tools:** Apollo Neuro wearables, Vagus nerve stimulators, and "Somatic Shaking" classes.
