# Is Stranger Things Over? The Future of the Hawkins Universe

**Dec 29, 2025:** As we count down the hours to the final episode of *Stranger Things* Season 5, a bittersweet question looms: **Is this truly the end?**

## Comparison: The Main Show vs. The Universe
**The Main Show:** **Yes, it is over.** The Duffer Brothers have confirmed that the story of Eleven, Mike, Will, and the Hawkins gang concludes with the Season 5 finale. There will be no Season 6.

**The Universe:** **No, it is just beginning.** Netflix is not letting its biggest IP vanish into the Upside Down.

## Confirmed Spinoffs & Projects
1.  **Stranger Things: The First Shadow (Play):** The prequel play in London's West End has been a massive hit and explores the origin of Henry Creel (Vecna). It is expected to transfer to Broadway in 2026.
2.  **Untitled Animated Series:** A Saturday-morning cartoon style series set in the *Stranger Things* universe is in production.
3.  **Live-Action Spinoff:** The Duffer Brothers have teased a live-action spinoff that is "1000% different" from the original. It will likely feature a new cast and location, perhaps connected only by the lore of the Upside Down.

## Conclusion
While we say goodbye to Millie Bobby Brown and the original cast, the *Stranger Things* brand will live on for years to come.
