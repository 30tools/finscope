# Laredo Update: Trade Booms and Festivities Begin

**Laredo, TX, Dec 29, 2025:** As the gateway for international trade, Laredo ends 2025 with significant economic news and community celebrations.

---

## World Trade Bridge Expansion
City officials announced today that the **World Trade Bridge expansion project** has secured final federal funding.
*   **Goal:** To add 8 new lanes to the bridge, alleviating the massive truck congestion that defines the Port of Laredo.
*   **Timeline:** Construction is slated to begin in mid-2026.
*   **Impact:** Laredo remains the #1 inland port in the United States, processing over $300 Billion in trade annually.

## Community: New Year's Eve
The City of Laredo is hosting its annual **New Year's Eve Countdown** at the Sames Auto Arena.
*   **Time:** Gates open at 7 PM.
*   **Entertainment:** Live Tejano music and a fireworks display at midnight.
*   **Safety:** LPD has announced increased DUI patrols for the holiday weekend.

## Weather
Expect a chilly start to 2026. Forecasts predict a cold front moving through South Texas on Dec 31, dropping temps into the 40s.
