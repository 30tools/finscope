# Fast Food Open on New Year's Eve 2026

**Dec 29, 2025:** Whether you need a pre-party meal or a late-night snack, fast food is a staple of New Year's Eve. Here is the rundown of who is serving on **December 31, 2025**.

## Major Chains
*   **[McDonald](https://unstory.app/food/mcdonalds-holiday-menu-2025-grinch)'s:** Most locations are **Open**. 24-hour locations will remain open through the night into 2026.
*   **Chick-fil-A:** **Open**, but usually closes early (around **6 PM** or **8 PM**) on New Year's Eve.
*   **Taco Bell:** **Open**. Most locations stay open late, perfect for post-midnight cravings.
*   **Burger King:** Open (Regular Hours).
*   **Wendy's:** Open (Regular Hours).

## Pizza Delivery
*   **Domino's:** Open. Expect high wait times; this is one of their busiest nights.
*   **Pizza Hut:** Open.

## Sit-Down Casual
*   **Olive Garden:** Open (Regular Hours).
*   **Applebee's:** Open. Some locations host NYE parties and stay open until midnight.
*   **Chili's:** Open.

**Warning:** Delivery apps (UberEats, DoorDash) may have surge pricing on NYE. Ordering pick-up is recommended to save money.
