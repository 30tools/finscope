# Stores Open on New Year's Day 2026: Your Shopping Guide

**Dec 29, 2025:** Planning a New Year's Day brunch or need emergency supplies? While many [business](https://unstory.app/business)es close to ring in 2026, major [retail](https://unstory.app/retail)ers often keep their doors open. Here is the confirmed list of store hours for **January 1, 2026**.

## Major Retailers
*   **Walmart:** Open. most Supercenters will operate normal hours (**6 AM - 11 PM**).
*   **Target:** Open. Most locations will follow regular hours (**8 AM - 10 PM**).
*   **Costco:** **CLOSED.** As always, Costco observes the holiday.
*   **Sam's Club:** **CLOSED.**

## Coffee & Fast Food
*   **Starbucks:** **Open.** However, hours are reduced. Expect most cafes to open later (around **7 AM**) and close earlier (**6 PM**). Store-within-a-store locations (like in Target) follow store hours.
*   **Dunkin':** Open. Hours vary heavily by franchise.
*   **[McDonald](https://unstory.app/food/mcdonalds-holiday-menu-2025-grinch)'s:** Most corporate stores are open; franchises vary.

## Grocery Stores
*   **Kroger / Ralphs / Fred Meyer:** Open (Regular Hours).
*   **Whole Foods:** Open (Modified Hours, usually **9 AM - 8 PM**).
*   **Trader Joe's:** **CLOSED.**
*   **Aldi:** **CLOSED.**

## Pharmacies
*   **CVS:** Many 24-hour locations remain open. Regular stores may have shortened hours.
*   **Walgreens:** 24-hour stores open; non-24 hour stores mostly open **9 AM - 5 PM**.

**Tip:** Always check the specific "Store Locator" on the official website before driving out, as hours can change due to staffing.
