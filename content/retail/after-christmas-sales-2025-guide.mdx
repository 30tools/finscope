# The Best After-Christmas Sales of 2025

The presents are unwrapped, but the shopping isn't done. The period between Christmas and New Year's (Dec 26 - Jan 1) is statistically one of the best times to find deep discounts on apparel and electronics. [Retail](https://unstory.app/retail)ers are desperate to clear shelves for 2026 inventory.

## The Big Hitters

### 1. Nordstrom Half-Yearly Sale
*   **The Deal**: Up to **50% OFF**.
*   **Top Picks**: Designer boots, winter coats, and Zella activewear.
*   **Ends**: January 2, 2026.

### 2. Amazon Winter Sale
*   **The Deal**: Daily lightning deals across all categories.
*   **Top Picks**: 2025 Planners, organizing bins (everyone is in "New Year, New Me" mode), and 4K TVs.

### 3. Spanx
*   **The Deal**: Extra **30% OFF** sale styles.
*   **Why Buy**: Spanx rarely goes on sale. This is the time to stock up on Faux Leather Leggings.

### 4. Target
*   **The Deal**: Up to **50% OFF** Christmas decor, wrapping paper, and toys.
*   **Pro Tip**: Buy your 2026 Christmas wrapping paper now for pennies on the dollar.

## Shopping Strategy
*   **Focus on Seasonality**: Winter gear is at its lowest price. Buying a coat now saves you hundreds compared to October.
*   **Online vs In-Store**: In-store clearance racks are messy and picked over. Online inventory is much easier to navigate.
