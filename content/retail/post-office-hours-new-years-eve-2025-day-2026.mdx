# USPS Holiday Schedule: New Year's Eve & Day (2025-2026)

As the year winds down, make sure you know when you can (and can't) mail your packages.

## New Year's Eve (Wednesday, Dec 31, 2025)
*   **Status**: **OPEN** (Modified Hours)
*   **Details**: Post Office locations will be open, but many will close early (often at **12:00 PM** noon). Regular mail delivery and collection will occur, but get to the post office in the morning if you need counter service.

## New Year's Day (Thursday, Jan 1, 2026)
*   **Status**: **CLOSED**
*   **Details**: No regular mail delivery. No counter service.
*   **Exceptions**: Priority Mail Express will still be delivered in major metropolitan areas.

## Regular Service Returns
Normal operations resume on **Friday, January 2, 2026**.
