# Target New Year's Hours: Yes, We Are Open

Planning a NYE party or need a "New Year, New Me" gym outfit on January 1? **Target** has you covered.

## New Year's Eve (Dec 31, 2025)
*   **Hours**: **9:00 PM Close**
*   Most stores will close early to allow team members to celebrate.

## New Year's Day (Jan 1, 2026)
*   **Hours**: **OPEN** (Regular Hours)
*   Most locations will open at their standard time (usually 7:00 AM or 8:00 AM) and close at their normal time (10:00 PM).

## Pharmacy Note
CVS Pharmacies located inside Target stores may have different, shorter hours on the holiday, so check ahead if you need a prescription.
