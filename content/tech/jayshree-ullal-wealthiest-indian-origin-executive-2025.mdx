# Jayshree Ullal: The $5.7 Billion Tech Titan

**Silicon Valley, Dec 29, 2025:** In a landmark achievement for women in tech, **Jayshree Ullal**, President and CEO of Arista Networks, has been named the wealthiest Indian-origin executive globally.
