# The "Eagle Jinx": Ian Eagle Apologizes on Air

**Ian Eagle** is one of the spectacular voices in sports broadcasting, but even legends make mistakes. During a crucial NFL Week 16 matchup, Eagle fell victim to the classic "Announcer Jinx."

## The Moment
*   **The Situations**: A kicker was lining up for a routine extra point.
*   **The Commentary**: Eagle noted the kicker's "perfect streak" over the last 8 weeks.
*   **The Result**: *Doink*. The kick hit the upright and missed.

## The Reaction
Eagle immediately owned it. "That is on me. I take full responsibility," he laughed on the broadcast. The clip instantly went viral on social media, with fans loving his self-awareness.

## A Family Affair
Earlier in the week, Ian made history by calling the Netflix Christmas Day games alongside his son, **Noah Eagle**. The father-son duo is quickly becoming a fan-favorite pairing for their chemistry and humor.
