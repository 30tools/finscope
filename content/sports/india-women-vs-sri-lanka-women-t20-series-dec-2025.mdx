# India Women vs Sri Lanka Women: India Clinches 4th T20I with Record Breaking Performance

**Thiruvananthapuram, Dec 29, 2025:** The Indian Women's cricket team continued their dominance over Sri Lanka, securing a convincing **30-run victory** in the 4th T20I at the Greenfield International Stadium. With this win, India has taken an unassailable **4-0 lead** in the 5-match series.

## Match Highlights

### The Mandhana-Verma Show
The highlight of the match was the explosive opening partnership between **Smriti Mandhana** and **Shafali Verma**. The duo put on a massive **162-run stand**, completely dismantling the Sri Lankan bowling attack.

*   **Smriti Mandhana:** Scored **80 off 48 balls**. In the process, she became the first Indian woman to cross the **10,000 international runs** milestone across all formats.
*   **Shafali Verma:** Blasted **79 off 46 balls**, showcasing her trademark aggression.

*   **Vaishnavi Sharma:** Was the pick of the Indian bowlers, claiming **2/24** and stalling the Sri Lankan momentum in the middle overs.

## Series Context
This series has been a lopsided affair, with India winning all four matches comfortably.
*   **1st T20I:** India won by 8 wickets.
*   **2nd T20I:** India won by 7 wickets.
*   **3rd T20I:** India won by 8 wickets.

The final match of the series will be played on **December 30, 2025**, at the same venue.
