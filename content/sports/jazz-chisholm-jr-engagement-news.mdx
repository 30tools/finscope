# Jazz Chisholm Jr. Gets Engaged in Winter Wonderland

New York Yankees star **Jazz Chisholm Jr.** just set the bar impossibly high for holiday proposals. On Christmas Day 2025, the infielder got engaged to his girlfriend, singer **Ahnalys Santiago** (Ahna Mac), in Finland.

## The Proposal
*   **The Setting**: Under the glow of the **Northern Lights**.
*   **The Ring**: Chisholm shared photos of a stunning diamond ring on social media.
*   **The Vibe**: "Magical" doesn't even begin to cover it.

## On the Field
While his personal life is peaking, his professional future is a hot topic.
*   **The Season**: Jazz had a historic 2025, becoming only the third Yankee ever to have a **30-30 season** (30 Home Runs, 30 Stolen Bases).
*   **The Rumors**: Despite his production, trade whispers are swirling. With the Yankees looking to shed payroll or package players for pitching, Jazz's name has popped up in discussions.

For now, though, he is celebrating a massive win off the field.
