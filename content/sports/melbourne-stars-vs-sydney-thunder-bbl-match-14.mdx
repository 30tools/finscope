# Melbourne Stars vs Sydney Thunder: Stars extend winning streak to 4

**Canberra, Dec 28, 2025:** The Melbourne Stars produced a clinical all-round performance to demolish the Sydney Thunder by **9 wickets** in Match 14 of the BBL at Manuka Oval.

## Match Recap

**Toss:** Melbourne Stars won the toss and elected to bowl.

### Thunder Collapse
The Sydney Thunder batting lineup never got going. Facing the raw pace of **Haris Rauf**, they crumbled to a paltry **128 All Out** in 20 overs.
*   **Haris Rauf:** The Pakistani speedster was unplayable, finishing with figures of **3/29**. He was rightly named **Player of the Match**.
*   **David Warner:** Failed to fire, departing for a single-digit score.
*   **Sam Billings:** fought a lone battle with a gritty 35, but lacked support from the other end.

### Stars Chase
In reply, the Stars made the chase look effortless, reaching the target in just **14 overs**.
*   **Joe Clarke:** The English opener continued his red-hot form, smashing **60 off 37 balls**.
*   **Glenn Maxwell:** The captain finished the game in style with a blistering **39* off 20 balls**, including three massive sixes.

## Standings Update
With this win, the **Melbourne Stars** move to the top of the BBL ladder with 4 wins from 4 games. The **Sydney Thunder** slip to 6th, having lost 3 of their opening 4 matches.
