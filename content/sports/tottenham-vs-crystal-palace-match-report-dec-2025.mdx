# Tottenham vs Crystal Palace: Spurs Edge Out Eagles in Tight London Derby

**London, Dec 28, 2025:** Tottenham Hotspur claimed a hard-fought **1-0 victory** over Crystal Palace at Selhurst Park, thanks to a first-half header from teenage sensation **Archie Gray**.

## Match Summary

The game was a tense affair, typical of a festive period London derby. Tottenham, looking to bounce back from a rocky start to the 2025/26 Premier League campaign under manager **Thomas Frank**, controlled possession but found it difficult to break down a resilient Palace defense.

### The Decisive Moment
The breakthrough came in the **32nd minute**.
A dangerous cross from **Pedro Porro** found **Archie Gray** ghosting into the box. The young midfielder rose highest to guide a header into the far corner, leaving Eagles goalkeeper **Dean Henderson** with no chance. It was Gray's first Premier League goal of the season.

### Palace's Struggles
For Crystal Palace, this defeat marks their third consecutive loss in the league. Despite the creative efforts of **Eberechi Eze** and the physical presence of **Jean-Philippe Mateta**, Oliver Glasner's side failed to register a single shot on target in the second half.
