# World Juniors 2026: Team Canada's Path to Gold

The holiday tradition returns! The **2026 IIHF World Junior Championship** is taking place in Minneapolis/St. Paul, and Team Canada is looking to reclaim global supremacy.

## Canada's Preliminary Round Schedule
Group B action takes place at the **3M Arena at Mariucci**.

*   **Dec 26**: Canada vs. **Czechia** (8:30 PM ET)
*   **Dec 27**: **Latvia** vs. Canada (4:30 PM ET)
*   **Dec 29**: Canada vs. **Denmark** (8:30 PM ET)
*   **Dec 31**: **USA** vs. Canada (New Year's Eve Showdown - Time TBD)

## Key Storylines
*   **The Venue**: Playing across the border in Minnesota means a hostile but energetic atmosphere.
*   **The Roster**: Canada enters the tournament with a heavy forward group but questions on defense.

## Where to Watch
All games will be broadcast live on **TSN** in Canada and **NHL Network** in the US.
