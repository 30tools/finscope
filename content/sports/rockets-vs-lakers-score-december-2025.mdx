# Rockets vs Lakers: Houston Blasts Off in 119-96 Rout

The **Houston Rockets** arrived in Los Angeles and delivered a lump of coal to the Lakers, cruising to a **119-96 victory** on Christmas Day 2025. The young, athletic Rockets were simply too much for the veteran Lakers squad.

## Player of the Game: Amen Thompson

**Amen Thompson** was the standout performer, showcasing his elite athleticism and scoring ability.
*   **Points**: 26
*   **Impact**: Constant transition pressure and lockdown defense.

## Game Recap
The game was decided early, as Houston used their speed to push the pace. The Lakers struggled with turnovers and transition defense, allowing the Rockets to build a double-digit lead by halftime that they never relinquished.

This statement win proves the Rockets are ready to compete in the Western Conference playoffs, while the Lakers have questions to answer about their aging roster.
