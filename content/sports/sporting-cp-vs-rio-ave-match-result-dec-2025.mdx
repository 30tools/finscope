# Sporting CP vs Rio Ave: Suárez Magic Lights Up Lisbon

**Lisbon, Dec 28, 2025:** It was the Luis Suárez show at the Estádio José Alvalade as Sporting CP dismantled Rio Ave **4-0** in the Liga Portugal.

## El Pistolero Strikes Thrice
The veteran Uruguayan striker proved he's still lethal in front of goal.
*   **34th Minute:** Opened the scoring with a volley.
*   **60th Minute:** Tapped in a rebound to double his tally.
*   **61st Minute:** Completed his **hat-trick** just a minute later with a stunning chip over the keeper.

**Maxi Araújo** added the fourth goal in the 53rd minute, capping off a perfect night for the Lions.

## League Standing
The victory keeps Sporting CP in **2nd place**, hot on the heels of rivals Benfica. Rio Ave remains stuck in mid-table at 11th.

**Player of the Match:** Luis Suárez (3 Goals).
