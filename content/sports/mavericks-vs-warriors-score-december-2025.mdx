# Mavericks vs Warriors: Splash Brothers Still Have It

The **Golden State Warriors** defended their home court with a **126-116 victory** over the **Dallas Mavericks** on Christmas Day. While the faces in the league are changing, **Stephen Curry** remains a constant force.

## Key Performances

*   **Stephen Curry (Warriors)**: 23 Points, led the team in clutch moments.
*   **Cooper Flagg (Mavericks)**: The rookie sensation continues to impress, dropping **27 points** in his first Christmas Day game.

## Game Summary
The Warriors used a balanced attack and superior ball movement to open up a lead in the third quarter. Dallas made a late push behind Flagg and Luka Dončić, but Golden State's experience closed the door.
