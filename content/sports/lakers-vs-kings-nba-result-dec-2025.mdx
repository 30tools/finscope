# Lakers vs Kings: Doncic and LeBron Power LA to Dominant Win

**Los Angeles, Dec 28, 2025:** The Los Angeles Lakers snapped their three-game losing streak in emphatic fashion, blowing out the Sacramento Kings **125-101** at the Crypto.com Arena.

## Star Performers
*   **Luka Doncic:** The Slovenian superstar was unstoppable, scoring a game-high **34 points**, grabbing 10 rebounds, and dishing out 8 assists.
*   **LeBron James:** The ageless wonder contributed **24 points** and controlled the game's tempo.
*   **Nick Smith Jr.:** Provided a spark off the bench with **21 points**.

## Game Flow
The Kings kept it close in the first quarter, with **DeMar DeRozan** (22 points) making tough shots. However, a 35-point second quarter from the Lakers blew the game open. By the fourth quarter, the lead had ballooned to 28, allowing both teams to empty their benches.

**Implications:** The win stabilizes the Lakers' position in the Western Conference playoff race as they look to build momentum heading into the new year.
