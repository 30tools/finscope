# Bowl Season 2025: The Calendar heats Up

The road to the CFP is paved with bowl games. Here are the must-watch matchups to close out 2025.

## Key Dates
*   **Dec 27**: **Pinstripe Bowl** (Penn State vs. Clemson) - A massive brand matchup at Yankee Stadium.
*   **Dec 28**: **Birmingham Bowl** (App State vs. Georgia Southern).
*   **Dec 30**: **Liberty Bowl** (Cincinnati vs. Navy).
*   **Dec 31 (NYE)**:
    *   **Citrus Bowl**: Michigan vs. Texas (A potential playoff preview for next year).
    *   **ReliaQuest Bowl**: Iowa vs. Vanderbilt.

## Watch Guide
Most games are on **ESPN**, with select marquee matchups on **ABC**.
