# Atalanta vs Inter Milan: Lautaro Martinez Difference Maker in Bergamo

**Bergamo, Dec 28, 2025:** In a tightly contested tactical battle, Inter Milan scraped past Atalanta with a hard-fought **1-0 victory** at the Gewiss Stadium.

## Match Summary

This was billed as the match of the weekend, and it lived up to the hype in terms of intensity, even if goals were scarce. Gian Piero Gasperini’s Atalanta pressed high, disrupting Inter’s rhythm for much of the first half.

### The Winning Moment
The deadlock was finally broken in the **65th minute**.
A quick counter-attack led by Barella released **Lautaro Martinez**, who held off his marker and fired a low shot past Musso. It was Martinez's 12th goal of the season.

### Defensive Masterclass
After taking the lead, Inter’s defense, marshaled by Bastoni and Pavard, stood firm against a barrage of Atalanta attacks in the final 15 minutes. Inter goalkeeper Yann Sommer made two crucial saves to preserve the clean sheet.

## Implications (Serie A 2025/26)
*   **Inter Milan:** The win keeps them **1st** in the table, maintaining a 2-point cushion over Juventus.
*   **Atalanta:** The loss sees them drop to **6th**, blowing a chance to break into the Champions League spots.

**Verdict:** A champion's performance from Inter – winning ugly when it matters most.
