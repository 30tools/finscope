# Cremonese vs Napoli: Hojlund Double Keeps Napoli in Title Hunt

**Cremona, Dec 28, 2025:** Napoli breezed past Cremonese **2-0** at the Stadio Giovanni Zini, thanks to a clinical first-half brace from Danish striker **Rasmus Hojlund**.

## Match Highlights
Napoli wasted no time asserting their dominance.
*   **13th Minute:** Hojlund opened the scoring with a poacher's finish inside the six-yard box.
*   **38th Minute:** He added his second with a powerful header from a Kvaratskhelia cross.

### Second Half Control
With a 2-0 lead, Napoli managed the game professionally in the second half, denying Cremonese any real goal-scoring opportunities.

**Key Stat:** Hojlund has now scored 10 goals in Serie A this season.

**Next Up:** Napoli hosts Lazio, while Cremonese travels to Juventus.
