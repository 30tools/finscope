# Mavericks vs Warriors: A Christmas Day Showdown

**San Francisco, Dec 25, 2025:** The NBA Christmas schedule delivered a classic Western Conference battle as the **Golden State Warriors** hosted the **Dallas Mavericks** at the Chase Center.

## Match Result
**Golden State Warriors 118** - **112 Dallas Mavericks**

## Key Takeaways
1.  **Curry Still King:** At 37, **Steph Curry** dazzled with **32 points**, including 6 three-pointers. His ability to space the floor proved too much for the Mavs' perimeter defense.
2.  **Luka's Effort:** **Luka Dončić** posted a triple-double (28 pts, 12 reb, 11 ast) but struggled with efficiency in the 4th quarter against the Warriors' defense led by Draymond Green.
3.  **Bench Depth:** The Warriors' bench, led by **Moses Moody**, outscored Dallas's second unit 42-28.

## Playoff Implications
*   **Warriors:** Move to **16-15**, stabilizing their spot in the Play-In tournament zone.
*   **Mavericks:** Fall to **12-20**, raising concerns about their consistency on the road.

The Warriors have now won both meetings this season, giving them the tiebreaker advantage.
