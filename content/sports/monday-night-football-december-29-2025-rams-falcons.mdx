# MNF Preview: Rams vs. Falcons (Dec 29, 2025)

The final **Monday Night Football** broadcast of 2025 heads to Atlanta for a crucial NFC clash.

## The Matchup
*   **Teams**: Los Angeles Rams (Away) vs. Atlanta Falcons (Home)
*   **Time**: 8:15 PM ET
*   **Network**: ESPN / ABC

## The Stakes
*   **Falcons**: Fighting to secure the NFC South title.
*   **Rams**: Clinging to a Wild Card spot in a crowded playoff race.

## Player to Watch
**Bijan Robinson** (Falcons) has been on a tear in December, averaging over 100 yards from scrimmage. The Rams' defense will need to stack the box to stop him.
