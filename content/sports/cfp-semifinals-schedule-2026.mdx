# College Football Playoff 2026: Semi-Final Schedule

The 12-team bracket has narrowed. The semi-finals for the 2025-2026 season are set for early January.

## The Games
*   **Fiesta Bowl (Semifinal 1)**
    *   **Date**: January 8, 2026
    *   **Time**: 7:30 PM ET
    *   **Network**: ESPN
*   **Peach Bowl (Semifinal 2)**
    *   **Date**: January 9, 2026
    *   **Time**: 7:30 PM ET
    *   **Network**: ESPN

## What leads here?
The Quarterfinals (played on New Year's Day) determined these four teams. The winners of these two games will meet in the **National Championship** on January 19, 2026 in Miami.
