# Jackie Chan Lights Up Italy: Olympic Torch Run 2026

The road to the **Milan Cortina 2026 Winter Games** got a dose of star power this week. Action movie legend **Jackie Chan** was spotted carrying the Olympic torch through some of Italy's most historic sites.

## The Relay
*   **The Route**: Chan carried the flame through the ancient ruins of **Pompeii** and along the stunning **Amalfi Coast**.
*   **The Atmosphere**: Crowds lined the streets to cheer on the 71-year-old icon, who looked fit and spirited as he jogged with the torch.

## Why Jackie?
Chan is a global ambassador for sport and peace. He has been involved in multiple Olympic torch relays in the past (including Beijing 2008 and 2022). His presence highlights the global appeal of the upcoming Winter Games.

## Movie Updates
Fans were happy to see him looking healthy. Chan also just wrapped promotion for *Karate Kid: Legends*, proving that he has no plans to slow down.
