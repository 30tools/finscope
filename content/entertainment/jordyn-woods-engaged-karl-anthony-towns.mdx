# She Said Yes: Jordyn Woods & Karl-Anthony Towns Engaged

Christmas Day 2025 brought extra sparkle for **Jordyn Woods**. The model and entrepreneur announced her engagement to New York Knicks star **Karl-Anthony Towns** after five years of dating.

## The Proposal
Towns popped the question in spectacular fashion:
*   **Location**: A private rooftop in New York City.
*   **The Setting**: Overlooking the Manhattan skyline, surrounded by close friends and family.
*   **The Date**: December 25, 2025.

## The Ring
Social media is buzzing about the ring. Woods was spotted courtside at the Knicks game on December 26th flashing the massive rock.
*   **Details**: Experts estimate it to be an **emerald-cut diamond** exceeding **10 carats**.
*   **Reactions**: Celebrity friends, including Kylie Jenner, flooded her Instagram with congratulations.

## A Power Couple
The couple has been inseparable since they started dating in 2020. Woods has been a fixture at Towns' games, supporting him from his time in Minnesota to his current tenure with the Knicks. This step marks the next chapter for one of the NBA's most beloved couples.
