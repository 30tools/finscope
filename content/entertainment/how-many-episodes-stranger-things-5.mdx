# Stranger Things Season 5: Episode Count & Release Schedule

The final season of **Stranger Things** is here, and Netflix has broken it down into a massive three-part event. Here is everything you need to know about the episode count and schedule.

## Total Episodes: 8
Season 5 consists of **8 episodes** in total. While this is the standard length for the series, the runtime of each episode is movie-length.

## Release Schedule
Netflix adopted a unique release strategy for the grand finale:
*   **Volume 1 (Episodes 1-4)**: Released **November 26, 2025**.
*   **Volume 2 (Episodes 5-7)**: Released **December 25, 2025** (Christmas Day).
*   **The Finale (Episode 8)**: Releases **January 1, 2026** (New Year's Day).
